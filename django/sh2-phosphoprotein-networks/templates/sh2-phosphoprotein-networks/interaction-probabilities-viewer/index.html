Interaction probabilities viewer

The MSM framework predicts probabilities of interactions between proteins containing SH2 domains and phosphorylated tyrosine sites, including ones containing multiple domains and multiple phosphosites. This website enables interactive exploration of these MSM features. Users can compute probabilities of interaction between known SH2- and phospho-proteins, mutant versions of these proteins, or entirely new combinations of user-specified SH2 domains and phosphosites. The overall protein-level probability of interaction is shown, along with the individual contributions of every interacting domain-phosphopeptide pair.
